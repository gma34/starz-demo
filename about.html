ABOUT US

Starz Aligned is a tight-knit group of artists and producers all united under a common goal, greatness. Our mission is to help producers and artists reach their goals.Starz Aligned came about in late November of 2022; the name "Starz Aligned" was chosen because it emphasizes our mission to align talent that would otherwise not know of the others' existence, aligning these talents to grow together. What started as a small Discord server with three members; is now a fully operative business. Starz Aligned's progress since its founding has been extraordinary, working with artists and producers, both big and small.We are very proud of our accomplishments since our founding, but we know this is only the beginning. Starz Aligned will one day be a household name, a well-known collective of artists and producers.
Starz Aligned is more than a dream; it's a promise. It's a promise between our members and supporters, an assurance of greatness.
